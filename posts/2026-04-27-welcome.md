# Welcome to My Research Blog

Hello, and welcome! I am currently a Physics PhD candidate at the University of Texas at Dallas, and I've set up this space to share updates on my academic journey and technical projects.

My current research focuses on the intersection of **multimodal geospatial foundation models** and **physics-informed neural networks**. Specifically, I am looking at how we can leverage massive-scale environmental and climate data—using tools like Google Earth Engine and high-performance GPU clusters—to better understand the drivers of human longevity and public health.

In this blog, I plan to share:
* Deep dives into spatial and temporal data imputation.
* Tips on optimizing machine learning pipelines (like LightGBM and XGBoost) on HPC systems like SLURM.
* Updates on my latest preprints and publications.

Stay tuned!
